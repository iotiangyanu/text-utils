About Text Utils

Text Utils is a smart and easy-to-use text analysis tool that helps users transform and examine text efficiently. It is designed for students, developers, content writers, and professionals who work with text regularly.

With Text Utils, you can enter any text and instantly perform the following operations:

  • Convert the entire text into UPPERCASE
  • Calculate the total number of characters
  • Count only alphabetical characters
  • Detect and count special characters
  • Exclude unnecessary or unwanted character pieces

The goal of Text Utils is to deliver fast, accurate results with a clean and visually appealing user experience.

Example

Input Text:

Hello World! @2026

Output:

  • Converted Text: HELLO WORLD! @2026
  • Total Characters: 18
  • Alphabetical Characters: 10
  • Special Characters: 3

This is just the beginning. More advanced features such as word count, text cleaning, formatting tools, and deeper analysis will be introduced in future updates.
